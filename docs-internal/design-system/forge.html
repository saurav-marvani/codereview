src/core/payment/refund.service.ts L142 Critical

Refund amount not validated against original charge

The handler accepts amount from the request body and passes it straight to the gateway. A caller can refund more than was charged. Clamp to the captured total before submitting.

- await gateway.refund(chargeId, body.amount);
+ const amount = Math.min(body.amount, charge.captured);
+ await gateway.refund(chargeId, amount);
